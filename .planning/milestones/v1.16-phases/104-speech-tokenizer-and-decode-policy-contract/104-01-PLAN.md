# Plan 104.1: Speech Tokenizer And Decode Policy Contract

## Goal

Introduce the speech tokenizer boundary needed for Whisper transcript parity without hardcoding
fixture text or token-piece mappings in Whisper kernels.

## Tasks

1. Pin the maintained `tokenizer-tiny.json` asset next to the Whisper fixture and teach the
   Whisper reference setup script to fetch and verify it.
2. Add a `speech/tokenizer/whisper` contract surface that records the pinned tokenizer asset,
   required control-token roles, timestamp policy constants, and suppression-policy ownership.
3. Add focused tests proving the tokenizer asset is present and that required role strings are
   resolved from the asset instead of invented in kernels.
4. Keep decode/parity behavior unchanged in this subphase except for contract plumbing; exact
   parity lands in Phase 105.

## Success Criteria

- `tests/models/tokenizer-tiny.json` exists with a pinned checksum.
- Setup tooling hard-fails if the tokenizer checksum does not match.
- Source has no hardcoded transcript text such as `[Bell]` or token-piece mappings in
  `kernel/whisper`.
- Focused tests prove required Whisper role tokens are backed by the pinned tokenizer asset.
