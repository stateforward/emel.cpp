# Plan 118.1: Public Runtime Harness Boundary Repair

## Goal

Move the maintained Whisper parity/benchmark EMEL lane off direct actor `detail.hpp` access while
preserving exact `[C]` parity and the single-thread performance proof.

## Tasks

1. Add narrow public component surfaces for Whisper model, encoder, decoder, and tokenizer proof
   code.
2. Update `tools/bench/whisper_emel_parity_runner.cpp` to include and call those public surfaces
   instead of actor detail headers.
3. Add a focused bench doctest that rejects future direct detail-header use in the EMEL parity
   runner.
4. Reconcile `POLICY-01` against source truth by narrowing the maintained policy to
   `timestamp_tokens` mode and publishing all policy fields in the EMEL compare record.
5. Rebuild and run focused speech/Whisper tests, the maintained compare wrapper, the
   single-thread benchmark wrapper, domain-boundary checks, lint snapshot, and scoped quality gates.

## Success Criteria

- The runner has no direct model/speech Whisper `detail.hpp` includes or namespace calls.
- The EMEL lane still drives encoder and decoder actors through `process_event(...)`.
- Tokenizer transcript publication goes through the speech tokenizer public surface.
- Compare reports `status=exact_match reason=ok` for `[C]`.
- Benchmark reports `status=ok reason=ok` with EMEL mean below the matched reference mean.
