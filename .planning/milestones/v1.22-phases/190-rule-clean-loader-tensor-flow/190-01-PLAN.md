# Phase 190 Plan 01

## Objective

Close the source-backed loader-to-tensor audit gaps without introducing a new loading strategy or a
second residency owner.

## Tasks

1. Replace the loader `load_tensors` callback seam with a tensor actor pointer plus pre-bound
   effect request/result spans on `event::load`.
2. Move maintained generation, Sortformer, embedded probe, and paritychecker setup to allocate
   tensor effect buffers before loader dispatch.
3. Route tensor bulk bind/plan/apply outcomes through explicit callback states rather than bulk
   dispatch-local `*_ctx.err` / flag fields.
4. Update focused loader and tensor tests for public actor/wrapper behavior.
5. Run the scoped quality gate with the generation and Sortformer benchmark suites.

<rule_constraints>
- Keep loader orchestration in `model/loader` and tensor residency behavior in `model/tensor`.
- Do not add a tool-only fallback or backend-specific loader strategy in `model/loader`.
- Do not update benchmark or lint baselines unless explicitly approved by the user.
</rule_constraints>
