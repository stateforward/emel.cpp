# Plan 147-01: Graph Callback Outcome Ownership Closure

<objective>
Close the reopened v1.17 audit by removing the maintained generator graph callback outcome bypass
and proving malformed generator compute readiness is owned by explicit guards and SML transitions.
</objective>

<rule_constraints>
## Rule Traceability

- Runtime behavior choice must live in `guards.hpp` and `sm.hpp` transition rows. Tasks 1, 2, 4,
  and 5 verify malformed generator compute inputs are classified before graph dispatch.
- Actions and action-called detail helpers must not route success/error outcomes through helper
  return values, `err_out`, selected callbacks, or mutated context fields. Tasks 2 and 3 enforce
  this for validation, bind, and extract callbacks.
- Detail helpers used by actions may perform already-accepted data binding or output copying only.
  Task 3 refactors the maintained callbacks to that shape.
- Closeout evidence must trace the maintained public path from `emel::text::generator::sm` through
  parity, benchmark, and embedded-size entrypoints. Tasks 5 and 6 record that proof.
</rule_constraints>
