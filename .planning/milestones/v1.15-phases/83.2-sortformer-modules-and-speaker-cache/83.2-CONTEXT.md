<decisions>
## Implementation Decisions

### Stage Ownership
- Put module projection/head binding and compute under `sortformer/modules/`.
- Put cache storage and cache mutation helpers under `sortformer/cache/`.
- Do not add a broad `sortformer/runtime` component.

### Maintained Tensor Contract
- Bind the exact maintained tensors: `mods.ep.*`, `mods.fh2h.*`, `mods.h2s.*`, and
  `mods.sh2s.*`.
- Validate the fixed f32 dimensions from the maintained GGUF summary.

### Cache Contract
- Speaker cache is preallocated and fixed-profile: `spkcache_len=188`, hidden width `192`.
- Cache helpers reset/write/read deterministic same-RTC state without allocation.

### the agent's Discretion
- The first module compute surface may be primitive projection/logit kernels, as long as it
  consumes validated maintained shapes and does not claim end-to-end Sortformer execution.

</decisions>
