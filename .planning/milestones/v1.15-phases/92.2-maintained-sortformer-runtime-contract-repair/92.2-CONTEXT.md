- Use the upstream NeMo inference path as the architectural reference:

  - FastConformer pre-encode produces `fc_d_model=512` chunk/cache embeddings

  - transformer encoder runs on `192`
  - sigmoid speaker head uses `first_hidden_to_hidden` then `single_hidden_to_spks`

  synthetic tests.

  repaired runtime.
