## Implementation Boundary

The executor intentionally consumes validated `188 x 512` encoder frames. It then runs the
maintained module projection, speaker-cache write path, and `te.*` transformer stack into a
deterministic `188 x 192` hidden-state handoff.

This does not fabricate the missing full Conformer encoder stage. The next phase must complete
the native feature-to-encoder-frame path before the milestone can truthfully claim raw PCM to
Sortformer hidden/probability execution.
