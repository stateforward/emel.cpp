## Decisions Made
- Graph owns the tensor lifecycle actor so reservation truth is centralized in one shipped runtime component.
- Lifecycle manifests remain explicit runtime payloads carried across graph phases instead of adding context mirrors or a second tracker.
- Generator pre-binds token/position storage before emitting manifests so reserve/compute calls stay truthful without reintroducing hot-path allocation.
