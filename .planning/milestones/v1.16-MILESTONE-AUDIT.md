## Source-Backed Maintained Path

The maintained EMEL lane traced successfully through live source:

1. `tools/bench/whisper_emel_parity_runner.cpp` maps and parses the Phase 99 Whisper model,
   binds model metadata from the GGUF loader, constructs
   `emel::speech::recognizer::sm<emel::speech::recognizer_routes::whisper::route>`, and dispatches
   public `event::initialize` and `event::recognize`.
2. `src/emel/speech/recognizer/sm.hpp` exposes tokenizer support, model support, route readiness,
   encode, decode, detokenize, success, and error outcomes in explicit SML states and transitions.
3. `src/emel/speech/recognizer_routes/whisper/guards.hpp` validates the pinned tokenizer SHA,
   tokenizer control tokens, ASR decode-policy support, and the source-owned
   `emel::model::whisper::build_execution_contract`.
4. `src/emel/speech/recognizer_routes/whisper/actions.hpp` dispatches speech-owned encoder and
   decoder actors through public events and detokenizes generated token IDs through
   `speech/tokenizer/whisper::decode_token_ids`.
5. `src/emel/speech/decoder/whisper/actions.hpp` and `guards.hpp` use decoder-owned detail; grep
   found no `encoder/whisper/detail` or `encoder::whisper::detail` dependency in the maintained
   decoder production directory.
6. `tools/bench/whisper_compare.py` and `tools/bench/whisper_benchmark.py` publish EMEL backend
   `emel.speech.recognizer.whisper` and runtime surface
   `speech/recognizer+speech/recognizer_routes/whisper`.

No synthetic, fabricated, tool-only, or reference-owned contract was found feeding the maintained
EMEL lane.
