# Phase 59.2 Plan 01

## Objective

Refactor the maintained embedding-generator model so prepare and encode behavior is explicit in
guards and transitions, not hidden in bool-returning helpers or generic runtime helper routing.

## Tasks

1. Add the phase artifacts and a failing regression that captures the current hidden control-flow
   seams in the maintained embedding-generator lane.
2. Refactor the maintained embedding-generator actions, guards, events, and state machine so
   prepare and encode outcomes are chosen explicitly by guards and transitions rather than by
   helper-returned bools.
3. Remove the remaining hidden behavior-selection seams in the maintained generator helpers that
   are still choosing runtime paths from inside `detail.hpp`, then rebuild and verify the
   maintained embedding tests and full quality gates.

## Verification

- `cmake --build build/zig --target emel_tests_bin -j4`
- `./build/zig/emel_tests_bin --no-breaks --source-file='*tests/embeddings/*'`
- `scripts/quality_gates.sh`

## Done Condition

The maintained embedding-generator source no longer relies on helper-hidden runtime outcome or
behavior selection for the scoped seams in this phase, the regression passes, and the actor
remains verification-clean on the maintained TE slice.
