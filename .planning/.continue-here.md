The Moshi sampler itself is still incomplete: it sorts raw logits, exponentiates only top-k values, and uses global `std::rand`. moshi.cpp instead scales all logits by inverse temperature, computes a full-card GGML f32 softmax, argsorts those probabilities, divides top-k probabilities by exponential draws, and takes argmax.
