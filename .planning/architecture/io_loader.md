# io_loader

Source: [`emel/io/loader/sm.hpp`](https://github.com/stateforward/emel.cpp/blob/main/src/emel/io/loader/sm.hpp)

## Ownership

`emel/io` owns loading strategy-boundary events and failure routing. It does not own tensor residency. `read_copy` dispatches to the injected `io/read` actor when available; mmap remains a separate `io/mmap` strategy, and staged/async/device strategies remain deferred.
